ABOUT SUE

Sue is a photographer living and working in New Zealand. She lives with her family in the Waikato region, located in the Central North Island. She specialises in capturing images that depict the beauty of the various environments in which we live.

STUNNING LITTLE BOOKS

Sue has created a number of 'little books' each designed to showcase a specific region or area in New Zealand. Sue's little books are beautifully constructed and comprise of images specially selected by Sue to best depict the heart, soul and individuality of the book's region of focus.
